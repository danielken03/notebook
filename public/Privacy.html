Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

This privacy policy describes how kenndoesdev.com handles your information.

What we collect

When you sign in with Google, we receive your name and email address from Google. This is used solely to identify your account and associate your notebook data with you.

How we use your information

Your name and email are used only for authentication. Your notebook content is stored securely in Firebase (Google's cloud infrastructure) and is only accessible by you.

What we don't do

  • We do not sell your data
  • We do not share your data with third parties
  • We do not use your data for advertising
  • We do not track you across other websites

Third-party services

This app uses Firebase by Google for authentication and data storage. Google's privacy policy applies to data processed through Firebase. You can review it at policies.google.com/privacy.

Deleting your data

You can stop using the app at any time. To request deletion of your data, contact us at the email below.

Contact

If you have any questions, reach out at hello@kenndoesdev.com.
